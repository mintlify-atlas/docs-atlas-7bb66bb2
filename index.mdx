Accrescent is a private and secure Android app store built with modern security practices in mind. It provides strong validity guarantees for every installed app, automatic unprivileged updates, and a frictionless experience — no account required.

## Why Accrescent?

Most app stores leave users exposed: apps can be silently re-signed by the store, update channels can be compromised, and there is no cryptographic guarantee that what you install is what the developer published. Accrescent addresses these issues at the architectural level.

## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Accrescent">
    Grab the latest APK from [accrescent.app](https://accrescent.app) and install it on your Android 10+ device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the app catalog">
    Open Accrescent to see all available apps. Tap any app to view details, screenshots, and version information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install your first app">
    Tap **Install** on any app. Accrescent downloads, verifies, and installs the app — no account needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable automatic updates">
    On Android 12+, go to **Settings → Automatic updates** to keep all your apps up to date silently in the background.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## System requirements

| Requirement | Minimum |
|---|---|
| Android version | Android 10 (API 29) |
| Automatic updates | Android 12 (API 31) |
| App archiving support | Android 15 (API 35) |
