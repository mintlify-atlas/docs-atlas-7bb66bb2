This guide walks you through the Accrescent app from first launch to having apps installed and updates running automatically.

<Note>
  Make sure you have [installed Accrescent](/installation) before continuing.
</Note>

## Open Accrescent for the first time

Tap the Accrescent icon on your home screen or app drawer to launch it. The app connects to the repository at `https://repo.accrescent.app`, verifies the signed catalog, and displays the available apps. No login or account setup is required.

## Browse the app catalog

The bottom navigation bar has three sections:

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **App list** | All apps currently available in the Accrescent catalog |
| **Installed** | Apps you have installed through Accrescent |
| **App updates** | Apps with a pending update available |

Tap any app in **App list** to open its detail page. The detail page shows the app's description, version, and a list of required permissions.

<Tip>
  Accrescent fetches only the metadata your device needs — ABI, screen density, and language. The catalog view is always up to date with the latest signed repository data.
</Tip>

## Install an app

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the app">
    Tap **App list** in the bottom navigation bar to see all available apps. Tap the app you want to install to open its detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Install">
    On the app detail page, tap **Install**. Accrescent downloads the correct APK splits for your device and verifies their signatures before installing.

    <Note>
      Accrescent uses a foreground service to manage downloads. You may see a persistent notification while the download is in progress — this is expected behavior.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the system prompt">
    Android will show an install confirmation dialog. Tap **Install** to complete the process. The app appears in your launcher once installation finishes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

No account, no sign-in, no payment information — installation is that straightforward.

## Manage updates

### Check for updates manually

Tap **App updates** in the bottom navigation bar to see any apps with available updates. Tap **Update** next to an app to install the latest version.

### Enable automatic updates (Android 12+)

On Android 12 and later, Accrescent can update your apps silently in the background without any user interaction.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap **Settings** in the top-right corner of the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable automatic updates">
    Toggle on **Automatic updates**. Accrescent will now check for and apply updates in the background using Android's `UPDATE_PACKAGES_WITHOUT_USER_ACTION` permission — no elevated privileges required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Automatic updates require Android 12 (API level 31) or later. On Android 10 and 11, you must approve each update manually from the **App updates** tab.
</Warning>

### App archiving (Android 15+)

On Android 15 and later, Android can archive apps to free up storage while preserving their data. If an archived app receives an update in Accrescent, you can restore and update it in one step directly from the **App updates** tab.

## View installed apps

Tap **Installed** in the bottom navigation bar to see every app Accrescent has installed on your device. Tap any app to open its detail page, where you can check its current version or uninstall it.
